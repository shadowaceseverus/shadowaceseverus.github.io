About me!

I'm a fanfiction author writing in the Harry Potter and Sonic Archie Comic fandoms, primarily about my favourite characters: Severus Snape, Shadow the Hedgehog, and Eclipse the Darkling. This year, 2026, marks my 11th year publishing fanfiction! I started on Wattpad in the Sonic fandom under ShadowAceSonic, but I moved to AO3 in 2021 and took up ShadowAceSeverus as my main username.

If you like a combination of slice of life, psychology, trauma, and character-driven stories, you should check out my works! My current magnum opus is The Courage to Live Universe, a canon-faithful continuation of the Harry Potter series with one major change -- Severus lives and pursues therapy in hopes of living a life that he wants to, instead of the one he's always had to.
